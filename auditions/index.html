Auditions

The purpose of these auditions at VCO is to get to know you better. Our hope is to accept everyone who auditions, and all you have to do is come prepared and show us that you will be committed to our process of music making. Our two primary objectives are to form a community of music lovers and to share the great music that we love. In order to fulfill our second objective, we must be fully dedicated to performing with our all.

If you need help with anything, including clef or a transposition, please email us at orchestra@vanderbilt.edu
