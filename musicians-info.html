Musicians' Information.



Here's the rundown on the retreat.
 
We will leave campus Friday, October 3rd, around 6pm and get back Sunday, October 5th, around noon. Put this in your calendar now! This retreat is mandatory and exceptions will be for very serious reasons only. We will be sleeping under a roof but not an insulated space. Therefore, prepare to bring or rent a sleeping bag.
The retreat will be an amazing time outside the city in the nature (or away from it if you choose). It'll be held at a sculpture garden/farm/nature reserve. If you'd like, you can even bring a tent and pitch it. There is a lake and a lot of wildlife like goats, horses, and of course, many plants and trees. We will have a big rehearsal on Saturday and many group activities, but the rest of the time will be free. There are trails you can hike, swim in the lake (questionable? or just canoe...), meditate in the forest, get a tan, do a lot of homework, play with the animals, tour the sculpture garden, and a lot of other things.
Audition Excerpts
 
 

Please download and prepare your excerpts:

  • Violins

  • Violas

  • Cellos

  • Double Basses

  • Flutes

  • Oboes

  • Clarinets

  • Bassoons

  • Horns

  • Trumpets

  • Trombones & Tuba

  • Harps

  • Percussionists, no excerpts. Positions will be decided within the section mutually with keehun

You should receive an email by mid-August to sign up for a 10-minute audition slot on either August 30, August 31, September 1, September 6, and September 7th. All either Saturday, Sunday, or a Monday. All of the auditions will be held at the Blair School of Music building.
